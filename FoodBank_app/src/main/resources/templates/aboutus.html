About Us

We at Foodhub, are working towards the UN sustainable development criteria : Zero Hunger. Our project aims to reduce world hunger by recruiting food donors and provide struggling working class families with a platform to appeal for food aid. Our goal is to reduce world hunger dramatically. Our project is a food bank application that allows users to locate nearest food banks and donate to them. This is our ultimate goal, to make it so that food banks will never be low on stock and are able to provide fresh produce.
